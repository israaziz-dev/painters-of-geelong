Areas We Service
Across Geelong

From the heart of Geelong to the Surf Coast, our professional painters deliver quality results across the region. Wherever you are, we've got you covered.

Geelong

Our home base. We provide comprehensive interior and exterior painting services throughout the Geelong CBD and surrounding suburbs with local expertise.

Newtown

Serving the charming streets of Newtown with heritage home repaints, modern interiors, and boutique commercial painting for local businesses.

Belmont

Trusted painters in Belmont for residential makeovers, rental refreshes, and commercial fit-outs. Quality finishes with premium paints every time.

Grovedale

Professional painting for Grovedale homes and businesses. From new builds to renovation repaints, we deliver lasting results at competitive prices.

Highton

Highton's go-to painters for family homes, feature walls, and deck staining. We understand the local style and deliver finishes that suit the area.

Waurn Ponds

Servicing Waurn Ponds with expert residential and commercial painting. New estates, established homes, and retail spaces — we do it all.

Lara

Expert painters serving Lara's growing community. Interior refreshes, exterior repaints, and new home painting for one of Geelong's fastest-growing suburbs.

Torquay

Coastal painting specialists for Torquay. Weather-resistant exterior coatings, beach house refreshes, and holiday rental turnarounds built to last.

Armstrong Creek

Painting Armstrong Creek's new estates and modern homes. Fresh builds, feature walls, and full interior packages tailored to contemporary designs.

Ocean Grove

Professional painting for Ocean Grove's seaside homes. Salt-resistant finishes, deck staining, and exterior repaints designed for the coastal climate.
